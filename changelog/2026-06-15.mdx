## Highlights

### Light mode

ProductBrain now has a full light theme. It follows your system preference automatically, or you set it from the menu. Dark stays the default and unchanged.

### A task inbox, not a backlog

Captured tasks now carry intent. A quick thought lands as a memo; a small, act-on-it item is a do-it; flag the urgent ones. When memos pile up, a guided pass turns them into real work or clears them out, so capture never becomes clutter.

## Other improvements

- Steadier AI drafting, fewer failed generations
- Shared plans got a one-click "make your own" and a share-forward button
