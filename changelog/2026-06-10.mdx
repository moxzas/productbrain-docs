## Highlights

### Curate your task inbox

Review tasks in Later, merge duplicates, promote the real ones to jobs under the right approach. A guided checklist walks you through each task with AI-powered signals for finding related work. Clean up the backlog without losing anything important.

### Drag jobs into phases

New two-panel phase assignment view. Later jobs on the left, your phases on the right. Drag to assign. AI auto-assign suggests placements for remaining jobs based on phase themes.

### Model explainer shows all three Kano tiers

The interactive walkthrough now demonstrates must-have, incremental, and delighter approaches side by side. Needs are framed from the user's perspective.
