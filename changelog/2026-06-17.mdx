## Highlights

### A deeper API for builders

If you drive ProductBrain from the terminal or your own agent, the v1 API is now a complete operating surface: read and mutate the tree, drive the live view, run the planning methodology on your own LLM, and manage share links and webhooks — with responses that coach the calling agent in-band (next-step hints, wrong-endpoint redirects, and a read-back of what each call did). The full contract lives in one place: the [LLM Guide](https://productbrain.com/docs/llm-guide.md).

### Email and privacy, handled

One-click unsubscribe on every marketing email, an updated Privacy Policy and subprocessor list, and a new plain-language [Your data & privacy](/trust/your-data-privacy) page that lays out exactly how we handle your data — including what we deliberately don't yet claim.

## Other improvements

- The task inbox got more reliable — one consistent classifier across the app and the API, and tasks you add by chat now arrive pre-sorted instead of defaulting to a plain memo
- Revoke or expire a public share link through the API
- Webhooks can now be updated and rotated in place, no delete-and-recreate
- Search and navigation responses describe themselves, so an agent always knows what it's looking at
