title: "What is ProductBrain?"
description: "A logic-driven product planning tool for technical founders and teams building with AI."
---

ProductBrain synthesises two established frameworks into a single bi-modal visualization. The same dataset renders as either a **Planning Tree** (strategy) or a **Delivery Map** (execution), with animated transitions between them.

## The Problem

You're building fast — features are shipping, but you've lost track of:

- What you're betting on and what you're not
- How each piece connects to business value
- What "done enough" looks like for each bet
- What to build next vs what to defer

Traditional tools don't help. Jira tracks tasks, not strategy. Miro captures ideas, not structure. ProductBrain forces visibility of tradeoffs — every decision you make is visible alongside every decision you're *not* making.
