## Highlights

### Agents that coach themselves
When your AI agent drives ProductBrain through the API, it's now guided to confirm changes with you before applying them, bring the canvas to whatever you're discussing — zooming to or pulling up a specific job and opening its details — and speak your terms instead of ours.

### Control your share links
List every public share link you've created, revoke one on the spot, or set it to expire. (Builder)

### More room on Free
The free node cap is now 100, up from 50 — more space to plan before you upgrade.

## Other improvements
- Dark by default — opens in dark now, with a Light/Dark toggle
- Rename a project's display name
- Milestone phases — bundle work for a launch alongside theme phases
- Agents can tell whether a view change landed, or was a no-op
- Cleaner Misc column and iteration handling
