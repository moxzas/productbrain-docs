## Highlights

### +Need button with AI placement

You can now start from a need instead of a goal. Describe what you're trying to solve, and the AI infers which goal it belongs under and places it in the tree. Most people think in problems and solutions, not business outcomes — this meets them there.

### Header redesigned

+Goal and +Need side by side as primary actions. Tighter, cleaner.

- Goals and needs auto-expand in Overview
- View toggle no longer wraps on narrow viewports
- Rootless needs handled gracefully during AI conversation
