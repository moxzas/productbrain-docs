## Highlights

### +Need button with AI placement

You can now start from a need instead of being forced to articulate a goal first. Describe what you're trying to solve, and the AI infers which goal it belongs under and places it in the tree. This is the most natural entry point for most people — you think in problems and solutions, not business outcomes.

### Header redesigned

+Goal and +Need sit side by side as primary actions. The header is tighter, cleaner, and the project selector matches the iteration dropdown styling.

## Other improvements

- Goals and needs with children auto-expand in the Overview view — no more collapsed dead ends
- View toggle no longer wraps on narrow viewports
- Rootless needs handled gracefully during the AI conversation phase
