## Highlights

### Task curation workflow

Tasks are loose ideas captured mid-flow. This release adds a structured pipeline to turn them into real work. Review your task inbox and the AI suggests what to do with each one — merge duplicates into existing nodes, promote real work to jobs under the right approach, create new branches for things that don't fit, or skip what's too vague to place. Tasks already assigned to a phase keep their assignment when promoted.

### Phase assignment

A dedicated two-panel view for organising jobs into delivery phases. Later jobs on the left, phases on the right. Drag to assign, click × to unassign. Auto-assign calls the AI to place remaining jobs by theme — releasable jobs follow their MVP siblings into the next phase.
