## Highlights

### From blank canvas to delivery plan in 4 steps

New users now go through a lightning tour: describe what you're working on → review the AI's structured plan → propose jobs → get suggested delivery phases. Four steps. No manual setup. You start with nothing and finish with a shareable strategy.

### AI suggests your delivery phases

After generating jobs, the AI groups them into 3-4 themed delivery phases. Phase names describe what users get ("In-store price checking"), not dev milestones ("MVP"). Must-have work ships first. Polish follows naturally in the next phase.

### Terminology refresh

"Iterations" are now **phases** — clearer, no sprint connotation. Kano "performance" is now **incremental** — plain language that communicates the concept without requiring framework knowledge.

### Project context from onboarding

The wizard infers your project's domain and overview from the conversation and saves it. Every AI interaction after that is informed by what you're building — not starting from scratch.

## Other improvements

- Wizard redesigned with confirm gates — AI waits for your approval at each step
- Phase descriptions visible at a glance, click to edit
- Explainer streamlined to 3 steps
- Error recovery — wizard resets cleanly on failure, retry from scratch
