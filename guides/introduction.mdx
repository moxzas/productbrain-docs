title: "What is ProductBrain?"
description: "A logic-driven product planning tool for technical founders and teams building with AI."

ProductBrain synthesises two established frameworks — Opportunity Solution Trees and User Story Maps — into a single bi-modal visualization. The same dataset renders as either a **Planning Tree** (strategy) or a **Delivery Map** (execution), with animated transitions between them.

## The Problem

You're building fast — maybe with AI agents, maybe with a team, maybe solo. Features are shipping, but you've lost track of:

- What you're betting on and what you're not
- How each piece connects to business value
- What "done enough" looks like for each bet
- What to build next vs what to defer

Traditional tools don't help. Jira tracks tasks, not strategy. Miro captures ideas, not structure. ProductBrain forces visibility of tradeoffs — every decision you make is visible alongside every decision you're *not* making.

## Who is it for?

ProductBrain is for people operating in the **complex domain** — where cause and effect can only be perceived in hindsight. You probe, sense, respond. You need to explore quickly because you don't yet know what works.

- **Solo founders** building with AI at breakneck speed
- **Technical builders** using agents (Claude Code, Cursor) who need a control surface
- **Product strategists** who want structured thinking without Agile ceremony
