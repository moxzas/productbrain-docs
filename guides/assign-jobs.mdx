---
title: "Assign Jobs to Iterations"
description: "Decide what ships when by assigning jobs to delivery slices."
---

Assigning jobs to iterations is how you decide what ships when. It turns your strategic tree into an execution plan.

## Assigning a job

1. Click on a job in the Planning Tree or Delivery Map
2. In the side panel, find the **iteration dropdown**
3. Select the iteration you want to assign it to

You can assign multiple jobs at once by selecting an approach and using the bulk assignment controls.

## What to assign

Not every job needs an iteration right away. A good rule of thumb:

- **Assign** jobs you're actively planning to work on in the next 1-2 iterations
- **Leave unassigned** (Later) jobs that are captured but not yet prioritized
- **Don't force it** — if you're not sure when something should ship, Later is the right answer

## Building a balanced iteration

A good iteration pulls jobs from multiple approaches across different goals. This ensures each release delivers balanced value rather than over-investing in a single area.

Check the Delivery Map after assigning — if your iteration has all its jobs under one approach, you might be too focused. If it has jobs from 10 approaches, you might be spreading too thin.

## Moving jobs between iterations

Plans change. To reassign a job, open it in the side panel and change the iteration dropdown. The Delivery Map updates immediately.

## Unassigning

To move a job back to the inbox, assign it to **Later**. It stays in the tree but drops out of active iteration views.
